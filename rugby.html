Our Top Fantasy Rugby platforms

SuperBru 9/10

Pros:

  • Covers major rugby tournaments: Super Rugby, Six Nations, Rugby World Cup, and more.

  • Multiple game formats: pools, head-to-head, and knockout.

  • Strong social features: create and join private leagues with friends.

Cons:

  • Lacks in-depth player stats and analysis.

  • Less customizable than some other platforms.

Fantasy Rugby Draft 8.5/10

Pros:

  • Offers a draft-based fantasy rugby experience (similar to fantasy NFL).

  • Strong focus on Super Rugby and international rugby.

  • Provides player analysis and expert tips to help with drafting.

Cons:

  • Limited to draft format, which may not suit players preferring salary cap or standard formats.

  • Only covers specific tournaments like Super Rugby.

Play Now

The Rugby Magazine Fantasy Rugby 8/10

Pros:

  • Covers northern hemisphere competitions like the Six Nations, Premiership Rugby, and Pro14.

  • Detailed stats and live updates to help with transfers and substitutions.

  • Active community and league creation options.

Cons:

  • Not focused on Super Rugby or southern hemisphere leagues.

  • Interface can feel complex for beginners.

Play Now

The Telegraph Fantasy Rugby 7.5/10

Pros:

  • Offers fantasy games for major rugby events, like the Six Nations.

  • Simple salary cap system that’s beginner-friendly.

  • Includes a dedicated app for easy management on the go.

Cons:

  • Limited coverage to northern hemisphere tournaments (e.g. Six Nations, World Cup).

  • Lacks year-round coverage for leagues like Super Rugby.

RugbyPass Fantasy Rugby 7/10

Pros:

  • Covers major international tournaments like the Rugby World Cup and Six Nations.

  • Offers prediction-based fantasy rugby, where users pick match results and key players.

  • Strong community features for creating and joining pools with friends.

Cons:

  • RNot a traditional fantasy rugby setup (more focused on predicting outcomes rather than managing individual players).

  • Limited league and team management options.
