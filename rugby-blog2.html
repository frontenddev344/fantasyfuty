Top Fantasy Rugby Platforms for 2024

Choosing the right fantasy rugby platform can elevate your experience and keep you engaged all season long. Whether you’re into Super Rugby, Six Nations, or global competitions, here are the best fantasy rugby platforms for 2024 that Kiwi fans should check out.

SuperBru SuperBru remains the go-to platform for fantasy rugby, covering major tournaments like Super Rugby, the Rugby Championship, and the Six Nations. Its simple interface and wide range of league formats make it a favourite among casual and competitive players alike.

Fantasy Rugby Draft For those looking for a more intense, draft-style fantasy experience, Fantasy Rugby Draft is a fantastic option. This platform focuses on Super Rugby and lets you draft players in a way similar to the NFL or NBA. The added competitiveness of the draft process is what makes it unique.

The Rugby Magazine Offering detailed player stats and a sleek interface, The Rugby Magazine fantasy rugby platform is a great choice for those focused on northern hemisphere competitions like the Six Nations and Premiership Rugby. It’s perfect for players who want a deep dive into player analysis.

These platforms offer a range of options depending on the type of league you want to play in. Pick the one that suits your style, and enjoy the thrills of fantasy rugby!
